Does your student org need cash? Create a short video about an IT service on campus and submit it to the ITS Battle of the Student Orgs for a chance at scoring the $500 grand prize for your student org!

Interested? Register and read the official contest rules.

Registration is open for fully-recognized student organizations on the UM-Ann Arbor campus now through February 28. Last day to submit videos is March 3. All eligible videos will be posted online for public voting, and a panel of judges will choose the winner. Submitted videos may be used to introduce and promote IT services across campus, including on ITS websites, social media, and at orientations.

What could your org do with an extra $500? Get filming!

Questions? Email Battle2013@umich.edu.
