World Class IT Research at Your Fingertips

I want to let you know about the easy access you have as a U-M campus community member to comprehensive information technology expertise, research, and analysis. Information and Technology Services (ITS) subscribes to research reports from Gartner, the Burton Group, and EDUCAUSE. The University Library also provides an IT Research Guide where IT-related information is easily searched and accessed. These tools are available at no cost to all U-M students, faculty and staff. Learn how to access these resources by visiting IT Research and Databases [http://its.umich.edu/resources/research.php].

Burton Group provides in-depth IT research and advisory services, including:

  • Research Reports
  • Methodologies and Best Practices documents
  • Webinars
  • Research Assistant, which identifies and delivers research based on customer priorities, current initiatives, and hot topics

The EDUCAUSE Center for Applied Research (ECAR) assembles leading scholars, practitioners, researchers, and analysts to focus on issues of critical importance to higher education.

Gartner is a global technology research and consulting firm with considerable experience working with institutions of higher education. Gartner research services offer:

  • Technology products/services/trends
  • Business-IT issues
  • Strategies/Governance
  • Case studies
The MLibrary Information Technology Research Guide helps you locate IT-related resources including books, articles, databases and internet resources.
