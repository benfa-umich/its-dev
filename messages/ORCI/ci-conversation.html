A CI Conversation with...
Dave Ward and Adam Gray from Amazon

Time: 1-2:30 pm
Date: Friday, January 27
Location: Ehrlicher Room
3100 North Quad building
105 S. State St.
(Enter the doors below the big tower at the end of Thayer, and go up the stairs or use the elevator off the lobby.)
RSVP: To help us plan for adequate seating and refreshments, please let us know if you intend to join us. Feel free to forward this invitation to any colleague or student you think would like to attend.

University of Michigan alums Dave Ward, Sr. Manager Product Development for Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2), and Adam Gray, Product Manager for Amazon's Elastic MapReduce service, will be leading an on-campus informational session on big data and scientific computing using Amazon Web Services.

Special attention will be given to Hadoop-style computation techniques using AmazonElastic MapReduce and Amazon's Public Data Sets. Time will also be allocated for questions and active discussion. Bring your scientific computing questions to this technically-focused opportunity to discuss what Amazon Web Services can do for research computing. Light refreshments will be served.
