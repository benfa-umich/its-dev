Welcome to the University of Michigan! We are dedicated to providing quality computing assistance to our students. Check out a quick guide to IT at Michigan.

Some important reminders as you begin your journey at U-M:

  • U-M Emergency Alerts: Register to receive emergency alert messages directly to your phone by text message or a call.
  • Safe Computing: Always protect your online financial, academic, and personal information. Never share your UMICH password with anyone, not even friends or family. You can authorize a Friend Account for parents or others needing access to your financial and billing information.
  • Free Anti-Virus Software: For your protection, we provide (and strongly recommend) U-M-specific anti-virus software at no charge to you.
  • WiFi on Campus: MWireless is the most secure WiFi network and should be used by all U-M students. Before using the service, you will need to configure settings on your WiFi device. How to connect to MWireless.
  • Computer Showcase: If you are planning to purchase a new computer or software for school, the Computer Showcase offers significant educational discounts.

On behalf of Information and Technology Services, we wish you a successful academic experience!
