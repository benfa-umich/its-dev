Mark Your Calendar for SUMIT_2013

The Security for University of Michigan IT (SUMIT) conference is an annual symposium hosted by the University of Michigan to raise awareness and educate the community about cyber security. It presents a rare opportunity to hear nationally recognized experts discuss the latest cyber security trends and threats at no cost.
