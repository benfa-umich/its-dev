Eye-opening. Refreshing. Informative. On Wednesday, February 26 from 2-3 p.m., ITS staff are invited to participate in a Google Hangout On Air with Laura Patterson on Connection Points. This event is an opportunity to hear from Laura and ask how we are doing as an organization, where we are with the IT Strategic Plan, and what we can expect for the coming year.

For more information, visit the event page on Google+.

Staff are encouraged to participate individually or as a team. If you have a conflict with service needs or scheduled meetings, speak with your supervisor to determine whether to reschedule—or perhaps repurpose the meeting for group participation—or to watch the recording when it is available Friday, February 28 on Backstage.

A Google+ profile is not required to view the event. You will receive an email reminder with a direct link to the webcast before the event begins. If you have questions, please email its-informrequests@umich.edu.
