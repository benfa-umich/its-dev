Action Required Soon! Change to Windows U-M Supported Antivirus Software

U-M's McAfee antivirus software license is expiring. Don't get caught with a Windows computer virus! If you haven't already done so, please download Microsoft Security Essentials (MSE) TODAY. It's easy and FREE! Go to Anti-Virus Protection at U-M [http://safecomputing.umich.edu/antivirus/] and learn how to uninstall McAfee and download MSE.

Spread the word to your fellow students. Installing MSE now will keep your Windows computer safe from viruses, even after you leave U-M.

If you already purchased your own antivirus software, you don't need to do anything further to have adequate computer protection.

Thank you for your help in transitioning from McAfee to MSE for personally-owned Windows computers.
