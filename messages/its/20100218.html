Learn About NextGen Michigan at Laura Patterson's Webcast

Please plan to attend Laura Patterson's webcast this Friday:

  • When: February 19, from 11:30 a.m. until 12:30 p.m.
  • Where: Michigan Union, Anderson Room CD
  • What: What is NextGen Michigan? What does it mean to ITS and ITS employees?
  • How: In person or at your workstation (Visit the ITS Intranet to learn how to view the webcast via computer.)

The Anderson Room can accommodate a small audience for the webcast. If you are interested in being part of that audience, please send an e-mail to its.communications@umich.edu. We will accept attendees on a first-come, first-served basis and send confirmation e-mails to those who are accepted.

This is your chance to interact with Laura face-to-face. Ask your questions — and get answers immediately. (You'll also be able to ask questions via e-mail if you elect to watch the webcast from your work station.)

We hope to see you there!
