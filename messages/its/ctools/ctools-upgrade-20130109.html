Happy New Year!

Have you discovered what's new with CTools yet? A new version of CTools has been released and it includes a new look and feel to the CTools home page!

On December 29, CTools was upgraded to version 2.9. This latest release is loaded with great new features, enhanced navigation and improved capabilities in many familiar tools. The 2.9 release is the culmination of several months of preparation and includes improvements to stability, security and accessibility.
