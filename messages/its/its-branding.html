Announcing New ITS Logo and Branding Guidelines

Hi Everyone,

I'm really excited about the future vision of NextGen Michigan and what it means for ITS and campus. As I shared at our meetings in December, ITS needs to change in many ways. It's important that campus views ITS as the preferred university shared service and not a separate and sometimes competing IT unit. Based on recommendations from the AVPLT, the Communications team, and others inside and outside of the organization, I have decided to transition to an ITS branding standard that better supports our future direction. Specifically, we want to position ITS as the glue for campus IT services and make our website a one-stop destination for all IT products and services, whether we provide them or not. Our new logo, which is at the top of this e-mail, intentionally emphasizes the university instead of the organization.

The new brand identifies ITS services more closely with the university's brand, leverages the goodwill and reputation of U-M, and focuses on the university and IT as one institution. Here are the main aspects of the change:

  • A U-M wordmark version of the ITS logo will replace the independent ITS logo. This style is the university's standard and preferred approach to branding, which all other Business & Finance areas currently use. Please stop using the former ITS logo as soon as you can.
  • Individual ITS services will be branded based on business content ownership or business partner relationship.
  • The M-Pathways logo and name will be retired and replaced.

You can expect to see the branding applied on a rolling basis over time as organizational materials need to be modified or updated. An overview of the brand and its applications can be found on the Intranet at https://backstage.itcs.umich.edu/comm/toolkit/. If you have questions, please ask the Communications team at its.communications@umich.edu.

In meetings, I receive lots of positive feedback. While our logo is important, what really brings a brand to life is the people. Thanks for all you do in support of our brand by meeting the IT needs of the campus community.
