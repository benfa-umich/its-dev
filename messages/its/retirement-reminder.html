ITS Retirement Open House

Don't forget! The ITS 2010 retirement party is tomorrow. We hope to see you there as we celebrate the beginning of a new phase of life's journey with our 34 retirees!

When: Wednesday, May 26, 1:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.
with words from Laura Patterson at 2:30 p.m.
Where: Dome at Arbor Lakes
and the patio outside the Dome
How to Get There: Parking is limited in front of and behind Arbor Lakes, so please consider taking the bus or carpooling.

Buses to Arbor Lakes will leave ASB at approximately 12:30 p.m., 2:00 p.m., and 3:30 p.m.

Return trips to ASB will leave Arbor Lakes at approximately 1:15 p.m. and 2:45 p.m., with the last pick up departing at 4:15 p.m.

You might also consider driving to the Park & Ride lot located off Plymouth Road (across from the U.S. 23 exit ramp) to carpool with colleagues to Arbor Lakes.

Many friends are moving on and heading in new directions. Come send them off with lots of thanks, lots of smiles, perhaps a tear or two, and best wishes for the future.
