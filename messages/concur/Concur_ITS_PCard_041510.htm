P-Card Reconciliation and
The University of Michigan Travel & Expense System

 

Beginning Thursday, April 15, 2010, ITS employees who are
P-Card cardholders, reconcilers, and/or approvers will begin using the University's new Travel & Expense system (Concur) to submit expense reports with P-Card transactions.

Going forward from April 15, all of your P-Card transactions are available in the Travel & Expense system and not in M-Pathways. The first P-Card cycle in the new system is April 15 – May 14. Please review the P-Card cycle to determine the cycle’s submission and approval dates.

  • Cardholders/Reconcilers: You can reconcile P-Card transactions throughout the cycle by importing transactions to an expense report and editing transactions, as applicable. You also have the option of reporting P-Card transactions related to travel on an expense report for the trip. Check with your unit to determine how to organize the reporting of
    P-Card expenses.

Reconcilers who act as delegates for cardholders will use the new system to notify the cardholders when the expense report is ready for them to submit.

  • Cardholders: You will use the Travel & Expense system to electronically review and submit the expense report containing your reconciled P-Card transactions.
  • Approvers: You will use the Travel & Expense system to electronically review and approve expense reports. Approval responsibilities have not changed.
