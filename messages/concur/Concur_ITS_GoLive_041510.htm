The University of MichiganTravel & Expense System Go Live

 

We are pleased to announce that today, April 15 2010, ITS is now live in the new U-M Travel & Expense system (Concur).

What does this mean for you?

  • All expense reporting (including P-Card reconciliation) occurs in the new system. Note to P-Card holders and Approvers: all P-Card transactions for the cycle beginning April, 15 will be in the new system.
  • All expense report approval (including P-Card approval) occurs in the new system.
  • Concur Travel (Cliqbook) is available for you to book travel reservations (air, lodging, car rental, and more).

You will use the system to:

  • Reconcile/submit all P-Card transactions and all out-of-pocket expenses incurred on behalf of the U-M.
  • Electronically attach or fax receipts for transactions to the online expense report.
  • Approve reports online, if you are responsible for that activity. Approvers will receive a system-generated e-mail notifying you that a report is ready for your review and approval.

Training

System training materials can be found on the Concur Training and Resources page in My LINC. Training resources include documentation, simulations, and employee and approver eLearning courses.

Getting Started

  • To Login to the system, navigate to Wolverine Access and click on the University Business link. Click the Travel & Expense link and enter your uniqname and UMICH (Kerberos) password for secure login.
  • Update your travel and expense profile to complete key information such as default ShortCode, default approver, travel preferences, etc.
  • Be sure to enable eReceipts to simplify your expense reporting process.
