Computer Showcase Celebrates Second Quarter Success

During the first half of FY17, Computer Showcase achieved $6.8 million in total sales, demonstrating overall growth for the year to-date.

Due in large part to the outstanding performance of the one-day sale event in November, second quarter Apple sales were up by 12% compared to the same quarter last year. As a result, Computer Showcase earned a growth bonus from Apple for the quarter, totaling almost $50,000 over and above regular revenue.

ServiceLink Peer Review Making a Difference for Depot

For just a few minutes each week, Depot team members audit randomly assigned ServiceLink incidents that were worked by their peers. The audit process helps both the auditor and auditee understand what quality work looks like in ServiceLink. The results of the audits are collected in a Google form, converted to charts, and shared with the team each week. The process has already helped the team identify and fix a gap in their processes. Learn more about Depot's ServiceLink peer review process.

Campus and Customer Happenings

ServiceLink Upgrades to Helsinki in February

The ServiceLink team will upgrade the current instance of ServiceNow to Helsinki in mid- February. ServiceNow requires upgrades to the cloud based system every 6 months in order to remain up to date with best practices. While every upgrade contains some additional enhancements, as well as bug fixes, we do not anticipate the upgrade to Helsinki to have much impact for end users. To keep informed on all things ServiceLink, including new features and changes included in future upgrades, be sure to join the U-M ServiceLink Google+ group.

ITS News

IQ Launches Data Concierge Pilot

IQ recently launched a pilot of Data Concierge, an ITS service that delivers administrative and learning management data in a consistent, timely, and secure manner. Data Concierge will provide a way for users to request (via ServiceLink) and obtain high-quality administration and learning management data in consistent, timely and secure manner. There are 3 types of requests: one-time data request, DataWarehouse enhancement request and data integration request. For more info, visit the IQ website.

Microsoft Surface Studio Coming Soon

The Microsoft Surface Studio will be available for purchase at Computer Showcase when it releases later this year.

The newest member of Microsoft's Surface product family, the Studio features a 28" touch-screen display that you can use like a traditional monitor or recline to create a natural drawing surface. The product launch video released in mid-December offers a tantalizing glimpse of the Studio's sleek design and innovative functionality.

Although a specific release date is not available yet, pre-ordering is open for the Surface Studio on the Computer Showcase website. The Surface Dial—a new input tool also compatible with the Surface Pro 4 and Surface Book—is already in stock.
