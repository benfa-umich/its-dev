Reminder: Reboot Weekly

Just a reminder that you should reboot your computer at least once a week. Recent changes to storage required a reboot to reconnect, so if you haven't rebooted lately, you don't have the latest access! Remember, rebooting helps with the performance and security of your computer.

Campus and Customer Happenings

Faculty and Staff Appreciation Day Sets New Records

Wednesday, May 17 was Faculty and Staff Appreciation Day at Computer Showcase.

Now in its 3rd year, the event was a resounding success. Total sales for the event increased 20% over last year, while advance pre-orders were up 25% from 2016 and a whopping 73% from 2015.

Between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m., Showcase and Tech Repair staff clocked nearly 300 total hours, serving 2,300 customers with the assistance of Tech Consultants and volunteers from across Support Services.

Best of all, the one-day discounts on Apple products saved U-M faculty and staff over $70,000!

Group Volunteers for Park Cleanup—You Can Too!

The Customer Service Delivery group in Operational Effectiveness recently volunteered to help clean up Allmendinger Park, near ASB. The group cleared an enormous number of invasive plants to help the 50-year-old lilacs thrive and refreshed the park sandbox, which got immediate use from local children.

If your group is interested in volunteer opportunities, the Ann Arbor Parks and Recreation Department was easy to work with and very appreciative of the effort the CSD Team put in. Let us know if your group has volunteered around the university or city!
