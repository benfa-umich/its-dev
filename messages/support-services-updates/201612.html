Banner Year for Support Services

Our accomplishments in Support Services are amazing! Each of you has contributed to the success of ITS on a daily basis.

  • People:
    Cross Tier Optimization Program — Engaging staff through connecting teams, building bridges, and developing career paths.
  • Process:
    Front Line Support Model — Leveraging Lean continuous improvement Kaizen event. Increases first contact resolution by 30% and reduced overall support costs.
  • Technology:
    Software self-service — Will reduce non-productive time by up to 4,500 hours/month. Improve customer experience.
  • Financials: Business Turnaround — Took over, stabilized, and grew the Computer Showcase & Digital Signage services to provide value add to campus.

Computer Showcase Breaks One-Day Sales Record

On November 30, Computer Showcase hosted their second annual Holiday Kickoff sales event offering one-day-only discounts on select Apple products. Total revenue for the sale exceeded all previous one-day sales, coming in at $1.2 million dollars and resulting in over $100,000 in customer savings. Special thanks to volunteers from across ITS who helped manage lines and ensure that customers received service as quickly as possible!

Campus and Customer Happenings

Campus Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) 2017 Strategy Revealed

Next year, we will release a new set of capabilities aimed to enhance delivery of services oriented towards those managing their own devices. Earlier this year, a team worked with students, faculty, and staff to understand the issues they faced when navigating technology, both at the university and in their personal lives. The team then worked with campus leadership to refine its scope of deliverables, due for release in 2017.

Read more about the BYOD deliverables coming out in 2017 and other MiWorkspace news.
